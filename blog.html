My first conference: HRI2020 Tuesday 19-05-2020

In March I was supposed to present my work in Cambridge and go to my first conference. Unfortunately, due to COVID-19, the conference had to be held virtually, and therefore made my first conference a lot less exciting. You can check out my presentation video here. I did found out that we were nominated for Best Paper in User Studies! Especially since this was my first research paper, this felt as quite an accomplishment.
